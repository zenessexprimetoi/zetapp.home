About Us

Zeness Exprime Toi is a youth NGO with the aim to bring a great and impactful changes in Mauritius. There are different sectors in which the NGO focuses on namely the education, health, environment,business, agriculture and technology. The members of the NGO are between 18 and 35 years old.

Our Beginning

NGO Zeness Exprime was founded in 2015 by a group of youngsters aged 18 years old. The idea of creating this NGO was to promote the youngster, express themselves and showcase their talents.

Our Mission

The NGO has a mission to help people in need poverty, give all people the chance for a medical check up(health), bring a non-invasive treatment for the premature babies (Octo-Project), provide tuition for the poor children (Education) and eradicate pollution to help the environment to grow better (Environment)

Our Vision

The long term vision of the NGO and it's dynamic team is that all its project to be completed in the near future.

Our Mandate

As the name 'Zeness' from the NGO name 'Zeness Exprime toi' means Young. All the members either executive and non-executive are aged below 35 years old.

Our Plans

The 2022 plan for the NGO is concemtrate more the Octo Project as it is the most impactful project for the community. The other project will also be taken into consideration like poverty, education, health and environment.

Stories

NGO Zeness Exprime Toi has received several national awards.

Health

The NGO has conducted several health event in stategic places like Camp Caval, Forest-side, Camp Chaplon etc. Databases from the events is used to draw a statistic.

Blood Donation

Mega Blood Donations are oragnised each year during the health events by ZET to help the blood bank to be sufficuent to provide the persons in need.
